Welcome to Defenders Ministry International Church

We warmly welcome you to join us in worship, fellowship, and spiritual growth. At Defenders Ministry, we are committed to nurturing a loving community where faith, hope, and love thrive.

Our church is a place where everyone is accepted, encouraged, and empowered to live out God’s purpose for their lives. Whether you're new to faith or have walked with Christ for years, you’ll find a home here.

"For where two or three gather in my name, there am I with them." – Matthew 18:20

Join our weekly services, Bible studies, and outreach programs as we grow in God's word and love.

About Us

Defenders Ministry International Church is a welcoming and Spirit-filled community, founded in 2005 with a vision to spread the love of Jesus Christ locally and globally.

Our mission is to equip believers to live victorious lives through biblical teaching, prayer, and compassionate service. We believe in practical faith that transforms lives and impacts the world.

Our ministries include children’s programs, youth fellowship, women’s and men’s groups, outreach to the needy, and more. Everyone is encouraged to participate and grow.

“Go therefore and make disciples of all nations...” – Matthew 28:19
